Todd has lived most of his adult life in the Lower East Side of Manhattan, home of NYC's hardcore scene. The neighborhood has been changing over the years, leaving many of its colorful characters, who made it what it is today, marginalized and left struggling.

Follow Todd as he explores his beloved neighborhood and see how it's changed through his eyes.
